ECON155: Intro Microeconomics with Nicholas Z. Muller

Professor Muller is a very effective lecturer. He is very good at conveying the concepts in the course. The size of the lecture class does not facilitate much discussion, but for an intro course Professor Muller is as good as or better than any other teacher.

FMMC285: Producing Environmental Media with Daniel E. Houghton

Daniel is an awesome professor and really knows his trade (animation). The class is definitely hard because everything depends on you motivating yourself to get your weekly work done. But, overall great class and awesome to see our final product come together over the semester.
